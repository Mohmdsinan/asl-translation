About SignText

Our mission is simple: give Deaf and hard-of-hearing people fast, accurate, and human-friendly access to sign language from typed text.

Why we exist

Many conversation tools convert speech-to-text, but real accessibility for Deaf people also requires translation into sign language — a visual, expressive language with its own grammar. SignText converts typed or pasted text into clear sign-language visuals (video or animated avatar), supports captions, and offers easy sharing so everyone can follow the conversation.

How it works — easy steps

  1. Type or paste text into the translator.
  2. Choose a sign style (live signer video, avatar, or simplified animated clip).
  3. Play the sign visual — captions appear below the signer for clarity.

We support multiple sign dialects and aim to expand local/regional sign languages over time.

Impact & values

  • Respectful: we collaborate with Deaf communities and native signers.
  • Accurate: translations are checked by human signers when possible.
  • Private & fast: text is processed locally/securely by default.
  • Open to feedback: corrections help us improve every day.
